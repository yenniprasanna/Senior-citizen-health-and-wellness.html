Nutrition Plans for Senior Citizens

Proper nutrition is essential for seniors to maintain health, energy levels, and quality of life. Here are some nutritious meal plans tailored for older adults.

Suggested Meal Plans:

  • Breakfast:

    Oatmeal with fresh fruits and nuts

  • Lunch:

    Grilled chicken salad with a variety of vegetables

  • Dinner:

    Steamed fish with quinoa and steamed broccoli

  • Snacks:

    Yogurt with berries or a handful of almonds
