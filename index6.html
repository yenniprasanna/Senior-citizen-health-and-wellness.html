Exercise Routines for Senior Citizens

Staying active is crucial for maintaining health and mobility in seniors. Here are some gentle and effective exercise routines tailored for older adults.

Suggested Exercise Routines:

  • Warm-Up:

    5-10 minutes of gentle stretching and breathing exercises

  • Strength Training:

    Light weight lifting or resistance band exercises (2-3 times a week)

  • Balance Exercises:

    Standing on one leg, heel-to-toe walking, and tai chi

  • Cardio:

    Brisk walking, swimming, or cycling for 20-30 minutes (at least 3 times a week)

  • Cool Down:

    5-10 minutes of stretching and relaxation exercises
